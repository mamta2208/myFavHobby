My Favorite Hobby
Swimming

Swimming as a hobby is surely one of the best. It exercises our whole body. People who swim regularly need not fear cardiac disorders or diabetes or arthritis- all the diseases that have crippled mankind these days. For children who swim, it ensures that their limbs grow properly and they also gain height.

Swimming keeps your heart rate up but takes some of the impact stress off your body. builds endurance, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness. It helps maintain a healthy weight, healthy heart and lungs. tones muscles and builds strength.
